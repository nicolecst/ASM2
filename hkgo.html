Project Overview

Project Objective

The project objective is to design a convenient and engaging mobile application which enhances the travel experiences for local and global travelers, allowing them to plan itineraries, know new friends, provide and gain insights.

Primary Audience

Tech-savvy users ages 18-50 who lack travel experiences or itinerary organizational skills and are looking for a tool to arrange their itineraries and gain insights.

Secondary Audience

Experienced travelers and small local businesses to make activities recommendations or promote their businesses.
